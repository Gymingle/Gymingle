🔐 Multi-Layer Verification

  • 15-second video check confirms real presence and blocks fake accounts before full access
  • Optional government ID verification adds a "Verified Identity" badge to your profile
  • Gym verification through local facilities adds "Verified Gym-Goer" badge

🛡️ Active Community Protection

  • AI-powered behaviour monitoring detects suspicious patterns 24/7 in real-time
  • Dedicated moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours
  • Clear community guidelines enforced with warnings, suspensions or permanent bans

🤝 Real Connections, Real Accountability

  • Every profile reviewed to confirm genuine, active person committed to fitness
  • In-app chat with blocking, reporting and optional location-sharing with trusted contacts
  • Regular profile audits remove inactive or suspicious accounts automatically

Meet the founder

Brooke CEO & Founder

Movement has always been part of my life through dance, sports and connecting with people through activity. After becoming single, I realised something strange: there was no place for health-minded people to meet naturally. Traditional dating apps focus on photos. Gymingle focuses on lifestyle and movement. Whether it's lifting, surfing, walking, or trying something new connection starts with shared energy. Gymingle isn't just about dating. It's about building a community where health comes first and real connection follows.

Gymingle is currently founder-led while we build our core team.

Frequently asked questions

What is Gymingle?

Gymingle is a health-first platform where people connect through fitness, movement and shared lifestyle.

How does Gymingle prevent fake accounts?

Every member goes through profile and video-based verification, helping ensure real people, real gyms and genuine intent.

Is Gymingle only for dating?

No. Gymingle is designed for gym partners, training accountability, community connections and optional dating in one platform.

Who is Gymingle designed for?

Gymingle is for people who take fitness, self-improvement and meaningful connections seriously.

Why is Gymingle focused on New Zealand communities?

The platform supports real-world meetups, local gyms and nearby training partners, starting in New Zealand, with plans to expand globally.
